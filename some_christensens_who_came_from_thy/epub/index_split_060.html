Orilla Christensen

(14) Orilla Christensen (1) was born 14 Aug. 1902 in Gunnison, Sanpete, Utah and died 22 May 1910 in Gunnison of scarlet fever five days before the death of her older sister, Inger Elosa, who died of the same disease. Orilla lived to be seven years nine months and eight days old. She was buried in the Gunnison City Cemetery.
