Adelbert Alfious Christensen

(5) Adelbert Alfious Christensen (2) was born 12 Apr. 1897 in Fayette, Sanpete, Utah, the same day that his cousin Elora was born in Gunnison. He died 29 Nov. 1904 in Provo, Utah of diphtheria at age seven years seven months and seventeen days. He was buried in the Provo City Cemetery.
