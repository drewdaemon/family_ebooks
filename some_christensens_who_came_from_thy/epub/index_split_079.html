Elsie Eliza Christensen

(33) Elsie Eliza Christensen (2) was born 30 Oct. 1908 in Salt Lake City, Utah. She died at seventeen months, 8 Apr. 1910, having been drowned in the fish pond at the family home in Salt Lake City. She was buried in Provo, Utah. Details of her death can be found in earlier pages of this book.
